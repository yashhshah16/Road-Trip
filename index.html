Essential Trip Tips
Book Meow Wolf ahead of time
Saturday tickets sell out. Purchase online before leaving Carrollton — it's the biggest splurge and worth every penny.
Pack a cooler for White Sands
Nothing to buy inside the park. Water, wraps, hummus, and fruit are non-negotiable. Pack more water than you think.
White Sands missile range closures
US-70 occasionally closes 1–2 hrs for missile tests. Check nps.gov/whsa the morning of Day 4.
Ask "Red or Green?" in NM
It's the chile sauce at every NM restaurant. Green is milder, red is earthier. Say "Christmas" to get both.
Don't skip the Turquoise Trail
The Madrid, NM detour on Day 3 adds only 30 minutes but is one of the most memorable stretches of the trip.
May weather note
May in New Mexico is excellent — warm and dry. Afternoon temperatures can hit 85–90°F at White Sands. Sunscreen is essential.
