Essential Trip Tips
Book Meow Wolf ahead of time
Saturday tickets sell out. Purchase online before you leave Carrollton — it's the biggest splurge and worth every penny.
Pack a cooler for White Sands
Nothing to buy inside the park. Water, wraps, hummus, and fruit are non-negotiable. NM sun is intense — pack more water than you think you need.
White Sands missile range closures
US-70 occasionally closes 1–2 hrs for missile tests. Check the park's website the morning of Day 4 for any scheduled closures.
Ask "Red or Green?" in NM
It refers to the chile sauce at every NM restaurant. Green is milder, red is earthier. Say "Christmas" to get both. Most dishes adapt easily to vegetarian.
Don't skip the Turquoise Trail
The Madrid, NM detour on Day 3 adds only 30 minutes but is one of the most memorable stretches of the entire trip.
Saturday Railyard Market in Santa Fe
Day 3 falls perfectly on a Saturday. Railyard Farmers Market runs 8 AM–1 PM with excellent cheap vegetarian street food.
